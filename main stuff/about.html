About SIMPLICITY

At SIMPLICITY, we aim to make the process of finding and reporting lost items as easy and stress-free as possible. Whether you lost a water bottle on campus or found a wallet at the local park, our platform bridges the gap between lost and found, quickly and effectively.

It features an intuitive interface that allows users to report lost or found items, upload images, enter detailed descriptions, and browse through recent items.Using our backend system, every listing is stored securely and made searchable in seconds, so users don't have to spend time searching but can spend time focusing on what matters.

Created by passionate students, our goal when we return lost items isn't just to recover items, but to build community, trust, and kindness. Every returned backpack, phone, or ID card is a step toward creating a more honest and helpful environment.

Join us in our mission at SIMPLICITY'S Lost and Found– It’s SIMPLY Lost & We Found It.
